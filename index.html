About the company

A DTC performance engine running at scale

MintAds operates a vertically integrated DTC supplements business processing 5,000–10,000 orders per day across European and North American markets. With over $100K in daily ad spend running entirely through affiliates and influencers — zero Facebook, zero Google — MintAds has built a performance machine that few companies match in scale or speed.

The existing Director of Performance Marketing (Sofia-based, Dimitri) built the function from scratch, but growth has outpaced a single operator in a single timezone. This hire is the second Director, tasked with owning Americas coverage end-to-end: campaign managers, e-commerce specialists, A/B testing cadences, multi-region offer management, and 24/7/365 accountability.

The right person is not a media buyer. They are a builder who manages builders — someone with affiliate-driven growth in their DNA, P&L instincts, and the leadership range to hold a pod of 6–10 people accountable at full operational tempo.

Must-haves for this role

  • 7+ years in performance marketing
  • 3+ years senior people management
  • Affiliate / influencer-driven experience
  • DTC or high-volume physical product background
  • Full P&L ownership
  • Team of 6–10 direct reports
  • Americas timezone (EST standard)
  • Available evenings/weekends at director discretion
