About the Role

Building a world-class performance marketing operation from the Americas timezone

MintAds is a vertically integrated DTC health supplement business doing over $1B a year. They run strictly through affiliate and influencer networks. Zero Facebook. Zero Google Ads. Processing 5,000 to 10,000 orders a day across North America and Europe, spending $100K+ daily on performance marketing.

This is a Director-level hire who will act as a builder who manages builders. The job is to recruit, train, and lead a team of 6 to 10 campaign managers and e-commerce specialists in a pod structure, own the full P&L, and drive a 20%+ efficiency improvement in year one. Always-on, 24/7/365.

What matters most: affiliate and influencer marketing at scale, strong executive presence, P&L accountability, and the ability to operate autonomously in a high-pressure environment. This is not a media buying role. It's senior leadership.
